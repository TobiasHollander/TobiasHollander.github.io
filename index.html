About Me

I'm a full stack engineer with a background in data analytics, bringing a uniquely analytical perspective to the way I build software. Before transitioning into engineering, I spent three years as a data analyst in the healthcare sector, where I partnered with financial and business teams to drive growth through financial planning and analysis and business development support.

During that time, I designed and built exploratory dashboards that surfaced key financial and clinical insights, and delivered ad hoc reporting solutions in Excel that helped stakeholders make faster, more informed decisions. Working at the intersection of data and healthcare gave me a sharp instinct for what users actually need from the tools they rely on.

Now, I bring that same analytical rigor to full stack development — writing code with the end user's needs and the underlying data in mind. I'm drawn to building products that are not just functional, but genuinely useful.
